Established in 2002, Saintgits College of Engineering (SCE) is located in Kottayam, Kerala. It offers NBA and IEI accredited courses, state-of-the-art laboratories, hostel facilities and infrastructure. It has a best-in-class Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in multiple streams such as Engineering, Business & Management and IT & Software. The Institute is a venue of national and state level seminars and symposiums and has emerged as the hub of technical education in Kerala.

It has a total of nine departments, including Applied Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering Department, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Robotics and Automation, Food Technology and Mechanical Engineering. The Department of Civil Engineering has become one of the main consultancies center in South Kerala.
